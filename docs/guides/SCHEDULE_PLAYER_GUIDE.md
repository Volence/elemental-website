# Schedule System - Player Guide

Your team's schedule page is where you submit your weekly availability, view team schedules, and manage absences.

## Getting There

Your schedule page lives at: `elmt.gg/schedule/<your-team-slug>`

You can also get there by:
- Using the `/availability` command in your team's Discord thread
- Clicking a schedule link posted by your manager

## Signing In

Click **Sign in with Discord** on the Availability tab. This links your Discord account so the system knows who you are. You only need to do this once per browser - the session lasts 30 days.

If you're already logged into the admin panel, you're automatically signed in.

## Availability Tab

This is the default tab when you open the page.

### Submitting Your Availability

The grid shows each day of the week across the top, with your team's time blocks down the side. Click a cell to cycle through statuses:

- **Empty** (no color) - Haven't responded yet
- **Green checkmark** - Available
- **Yellow question mark** - Maybe / uncertain

Click a day header to fill the entire column at once. It cycles: all available, all maybe, all clear.

When you're done, hit **Save**.

### "Not Available This Week"

If you can't make any sessions this week, click **Not Available This Week** instead of leaving everything blank. This lets your manager know you've seen the calendar but can't attend, rather than wondering if you just forgot to respond.

### Notes

Use the notes field to add context - "available but prefer later blocks", "might be 15 min late Tuesday", etc. Your manager sees these when building lineups.

### Switching Weeks

If it's Friday or later, you'll see a week switcher at the top letting you toggle between the current week and next week. Submit availability for both if you can.

### Viewing Team Availability

Below your voting grid is the **Team Availability Matrix**. This shows everyone's responses in one view:

- Each row is a team member with their role badge
- Green = available, yellow = maybe, gray strikethrough = absent
- The bottom row shows availability counts per slot
- Use the filter buttons at the top to focus on a specific time block

## Calendar Tab

The Calendar tab has two sections:

### Month View

Browse past and future weeks. Click any week to see what the schedule looked like - who was available, what scrims were set up, results, etc.

### Absences

Below the calendar, you can manage your absences:

- Click **Add Absence** to set a date range when you'll be unavailable
- Optionally add a reason ("vacation", "exams", etc.)
- Absences automatically gray out those days on your availability grid
- Your manager can see all team absences at a glance
- Delete an absence if your plans change

Absences are different from weekly availability - they're for longer stretches (travel, exams, breaks) so your manager can plan ahead.

## Tips

- **Submit early.** Your manager builds lineups from availability data. Earlier responses = better lineups.
- **Update if things change.** You can re-submit availability as many times as you want before the schedule is built.
- **Use "maybe" honestly.** It helps managers know they might need a backup for that slot.
- **Set absences in advance.** If you know you'll be gone in two weeks, set it now so your manager can plan.
